The slaps dichotomy

Slaps is a metaphor of life. Sometimes life slaps, and sometimes life slaps you.

The stoic in all of us is inherently aware that life is a series of ups and downs, undulating between euphoria and hard times. Slaps represents both — embracing the times when you're on top of your game, and equally when you’ve been dealt a dose of reality.

When you slap, be proud but not complacent; if life slaps you, get back up and go again. The bigger the slap, the more epic the comeback.

In all of this, it's essential we don't forget… sometimes you just have to slap some ass. Sometimes life be that simple.
