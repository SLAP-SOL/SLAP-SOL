Origin of the Slap!

Slaps is a metaphor of life. Sometimes life slaps, and sometimes life slaps you.

The stoic in all of us is inherently aware that life is a series of ups and downs, undulating between euphoria and hard times. Slaps represents both, embracing the times when you're on top of your game, and equally when we've been dealt a dose of reality. Slaps coin is the currency of character, how you deal with life's slaps defines you and your story.

When you slap, be proud but not complacent, if life's slapped you, get back up and go again. The bigger the slap the more epic the comeback. No matter how many times we get slapped down, we are relentless in our pursuit of being better.

In all of this, it's essential we don't forget.. sometimes you just have to slap some ass. Sometimes life be that simple.
